  • DISCOVER YOGA

    Yoga path to freedom, peace, and balance

    Through delightful insights, direct instruction, postural alignment, pranayama, and awareness meditation discover the dissolution of illusory separateness as you experience a reunification of your physical and spiritual consciousness.


    Why join?

    Workshops with this dynamic teaching include Asana, philosophy, pranayama and meditation and often lead to profound personal insights and lasting transformation. Learn more.

    Meet trainer

    Through his training, Pukesh aims to clearly explain the what, why’s and how’s of asana, meditation and yogic philosophy making the practice approachable for everyone, of every level and of every walk of life.
